New York, often called New York City (NYC) to distinguish it from the state of New York, is the most populous city in the United States.
With a 2020 population of 8,804,190 distributed over 300.46 square miles (778.2 km2),
Located at the southern tip of the state of New York,
the city is the center23 of the New York metropolitan area, the largest metropolitan area in the world by urban area.
